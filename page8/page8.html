CROFT COMPACTOR / DE-WATERING PRESSES FOR PAPER MILL REJECT:

These compactors are built with our patented "Non - Jamming" screws, designed to prevent blocking of the screw.

CROFT REJECT TROMMELS:





We manufacture a Modular framed Reject Trommel for any Paper Mill Reject. We can offer both a positive or a negative angle. Our Trommels also feature Tumbler bars rather than a screw which results in more efficient washing and reduced fibre loss.

CROFT "NON BLOCKING" DIRT TRAPS:

The "Maag Non - Blocking" dirt trap has a unique design which prevents the lower valve (door) from blocking during its cycle. (Suitable for Pulpers, Cleaners and any dirt removal applications).

CROFT SAND SEPARATORS

The Croft Sand Separator allows for the removal of sand and heavy deposits from any backwater system. The machine is a non pressurised machine, which removes the sand via a screw feed.
